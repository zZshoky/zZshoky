Regulatory Edge

The window is open — and PR has the fast lane

Argentina's Milei government dismantled a decade of import barriers in 14 months. Puerto Rico-origin coffee now holds a regulatory speed advantage over every competing origin.

Dec 2023

SIRA eliminated

Import licensing (LA/LNA) abolished. No prior authorization system.

Dec 23, 2024

Impuesto PAIS expires

−7.5% cost reduction on all imports. Gone permanently.

Jan 20, 2025

Decreto 35/2025 — PR's fast lane

US (incl. Puerto Rico) classified as Annex III. No RNE or RNPA registration needed. ANMAT approves in 48 hours via Declaración Jurada. Colombia and Ethiopia still need the slow track.

Feb 26, 2025

SEDI derogated entirely

No import authorization system exists. Free import regime.

Apr 14, 2025

Cepo cambiario lifted

Free dollar purchases. Argentine importers can remit dividends freely.

🇵🇷 PR Export Side

  • FDA facility registration — Section 415, biennial, free
  • APHIS PPQ-577 — phytosanitary certificate via PCIT
  • EEI via AES — required for goods >$2,500
  • Act 60 Ch. 3 — 4% corporate tax on export income
  • Certificate of Origin — legalized by Argentine Consulate

🇦🇷 Argentina Import Side

  • ARCA importer profile — one-time registration
  • SENASA AFIDI — 60-day validity, pre-shipment, via SIGPV-IMPO
  • ANMAT DJ — 48-hr fast track (Annex III/US origin)
  • No RNE/RNPA needed — eliminated by Decreto 35/2025
  • ISPM-15 pallets — heat-treated markings required
